How Does This Work?

Compete against different teams from various colleges across Mumbai. It is an OFFLINE MODE Hackathon

1. Choose A Domain

Pick a domain from one of our tracks: Smart Cities or FinTech. Each domain gives you some info, but you’re encouraged to research the problem yourself!

2. Ideate Efficient Solutions

Once you’ve got a grasp on the problem, dig into your brains! Create flow of execution or directly start working on the prototype, whichever works best for you!

3. Compete With Other Teams OFFLINE

After you’ve made efficient solutions, bring it up to high-fidelity. Be confident and nail your judging rounds to defeat other competitors.

4. Submit Your Solution to Earn Prizes!!

Submit your prototype and present it to our esteemed judges and they will judge your work. You might just win a prize!

Domains

Select whichever domain you like and you will get a problem statement based on it for which you have to work for 24+ hours.
