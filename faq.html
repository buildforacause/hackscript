FAQ

If there is an answer you cannot find, please let us know on apsit@apsit.edu.in

Who can register?

keyboard_arrow_down

Anyone! This event is open to all engineering students pursuing a Bachelor's Degree - and it’s all free!

I’m new to Coding, can I participate?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes! Experience leads to learning. Explore the brilliant brains taking part in this hackathon to learn and treasure for two days on our campus.

Do we need to form a team?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, we want to challenge both your thinking and coding skills. Teams can be between 2 to 4 participants. Most participants choose a team of 4. You need to make a team at the time of registration.

How much does the HackScript cost?

keyboard_arrow_down

HackScript by A.P.Shah Institute Of Technology is free for any current engineering student to join!

What is the code of conduct?

keyboard_arrow_down

This is an educational experience and we want you to learn as much as you can. We request you abide by the following rules:
‍
1. No seeking professional help from anyone outside of the Protothon. We have mentors at the event ready to help you!
‍
2. No registrations will be accepted after deadline (March 13 2023, 11:59 PM)
‍
3. Out of respect, for your fellow participants, judges, and organizers, please keep yourself on mute during the opening ceremony, presentations, results, and closing ceremony.

How do I choose a domain?

keyboard_arrow_down

At the day of registration, you can choose one domain from the two choices: Smart Cities or FinTech. Once you have chosen a domain, you will get a problem statement when you reach here in A.P.Shah Institute Of Technology for HackScript. You need to work on that problem statement and build expected solutions.

Is there a Participation Certificate?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes! Each and every individual who accepts to be a participant of HackScript and works here for the hackathon period, will get a Certificate of Participation. If you're skilled enough, you may also win prizes from our 50k prizepool!

Are we free to use any open source technology?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes. You are free to use any open source technology and build effective solutions.

Are there prizes?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, there are prizes for both the domains! Each Winner from the domain will get INR 15,000 and Runner-Ups will get INR 10,000.

What if I need to drop out for some reason?

keyboard_arrow_down

Dont Worry! We will send a confirmation email to each team leader who needs to confirm their participation. If failed to do so, you won't be able to participate! For any further queries contact apsit@apsit.edu.in
